# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

## OctoAcme Project Management – Overview

OctoAcme follows a clear and structured project management approach designed to deliver customer value through iterative development and strong team collaboration.

### Project Lifecycle
OctoAcme projects progress through five distinct phases:
1. **Initiation** – Define project scope, objectives, and stakeholder alignment
2. **Planning** – Develop detailed roadmaps, timelines, and resource allocation
3. **Execution** – Implement features, track progress, and manage day-to-day activities
4. **Release** – Deploy to production with quality assurance and stakeholder sign-off
5. **Retrospective** – Reflect on outcomes and identify continuous improvements

### Core Principles
- **Customer Value First** – Every project prioritizes delivering measurable value to end users
- **Iterative Delivery** – Work is completed in increments to enable early feedback and course correction
- **Strong Ownership** – Clear roles and accountability ensure project success
- **Data-Informed Decisions** – Metrics and feedback guide planning and execution decisions

### Team Roles
- **Project Manager** – Oversees timeline, scope, and stakeholder communication
- **Product Manager** – Defines vision, priorities, and customer requirements
- **Developers** – Build features and technical solutions
- **QA/Testing** – Ensure quality and validate deliverables
- **Stakeholders** – Provide requirements, approval, and business context

### Key Artifacts & Tools
- Project Charter and Business Case
- Product Roadmap
- Work Backlog and Sprint Plans
- Risk Register
- Release Plan
- Retrospective Reports
